The Beginning

The Grand Hotel opened its doors in the roaring twenties. Designed by the visionary architect Henri LeGrand, it quickly became the sanctuary for artists, diplomats, and royalty. The marble floors and crystal chandeliers whisper stories of gala nights and secret meetings that shaped the history of our city.

The Golden Era

Through the decades, we have maintained our commitment to excellence. In the 1960s, the hotel underwent a major renovation to include modern luxuries while preserving its Art Deco soul. It was here that the famous "Midnight Jazz Sessions" were born, attracting musicians from around the globe.

Defining Modern Luxury

Today, under the careful stewardship of the Belgacem Family, the Grand Hotel continues to set the standard. We believe luxury is in the details—the crispness of the linen, the warmth of the greeting, and the seamless fusion of heritage and innovation.
